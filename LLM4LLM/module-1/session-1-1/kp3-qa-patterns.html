Deep Insights

  • No special Q&A programming: Question answering emerges from general pattern recognition
  • Context matters: The model uses surrounding text to determine what kind of response is expected
  • Probability-based: Answers with higher probability in similar contexts are more likely
  • Limitation: The model can only "know" what was in its training data
  • Generalization: Can handle new questions by combining learned patterns
